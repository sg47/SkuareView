Synopsis

Measures the area of the region, along with the location of its centroid. This information can be combined to determine area and centroid values for any larger collection of ROI regions.

As with measure_span, this function considers that every grid point within the region spans +/-0.5 from its centre, so that a rectangular region which contains exactly one grid point has an area of 1.0, as opposed to 0.0. Similar considerations are applied in measure the area of all region shapes.
