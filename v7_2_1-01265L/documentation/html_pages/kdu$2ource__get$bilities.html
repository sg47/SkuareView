Synopsis

The returned capabilities word always includes the flag, KDU_SOURCE_CAP_SEQUENTIAL, but may also include KDU_SOURCE_CAP_SEEKABLE, depending on the allow_seeks argument passed to open. See kdu_compressed_source::get_capabilities for an explanation of capabilities.
