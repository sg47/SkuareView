Synopsis

Same as convert_rgb, except that an extra line buffer is supplied. This allows all 4 channels of a 4-channel colour space to be converted to RGB. Upon return, the red, green and blue line buffers contain converted RGB data, whereas the contents of the extra line buffer are left unchanged.
