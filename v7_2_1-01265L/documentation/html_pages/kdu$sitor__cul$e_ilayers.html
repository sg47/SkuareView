Synopsis

This function may be used to remove some of the least recently used ilayers from the internal inactive list. Imagery layers are added to this list when remove_ilayer is called with a permanent argument of false. Old ilayers are also moved to the inactive list when set_frame is used to advance to a new frame in a JPX animation.

Arguments

max_inactive [int]

Number of most recently active ilayers to leave on the inactive list.
